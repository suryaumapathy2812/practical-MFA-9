## Colors

- Blue: hsl(212, 100%, 50%)
- Light blue: hsl(217, 20%, 51%)
- Dark blue: hsl(217, 35%, 45%)
- Dark blue (darker): hsl(222, 41%, 20%)
- Very dark blue: hsl(217, 21%, 21%)
- Very dark blue (darker): hsl(220, 40%, 13%)
- Very light blue: hsl(227, 100%, 98%)
- White: hsl(0, 0%, 100%)
